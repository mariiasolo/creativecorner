Project

YTK company

Case requirements

The aim of the project is to create a new way to become a student member. Any channels and services can be used.

No need to take into consideration the current way of filling in the form on the YTK website.

Project steps

Introduction

Goal: Make user sign-up including the authentication process easier and quicker, and thus attract more users to join YTK services.

Attracting as many students as possible is the focus for the YTK company. However, it is out of the scope for Creative Corner Team during the Service Design course.

That is why, Creative Corner delivers solution on how to simplify and make the enrollment process more attractive and fun, so that more users would be joining the system with few clicks.

UserScenarios

Steps needed to make intentions of the users achievable:

  • Prerequisite: Students have Tuudo app. (does not depend on YTK or anyone else)
  • To spread an add on social media about YTK so that more students aware of it (not in the scope in this case project)
  • To integrate an YTK service into Tuudo
  • To automate pop-up windows for students depending on the their status such as newbies, students that already watched a video and clicked “remind me later”, students that have joined the student service see a reminder to join YTK Fund services once becoming employed.
